---
title: Organizations and same-domain invites
date: 2026-05-08
version: '0.12.0'
tags: [feature]
---
Every user is now part of an organization. Users with verified work-domain emails (e.g. anyone on `@yourcompany.com`) share one workspace and can invite teammates with matching domains via the new `/team` page. Users on generic free-mail domains (gmail, outlook, etc.) get a personal workspace and don't see invitations. Resources stay private to each user — orgs only own identity and invites for now.
