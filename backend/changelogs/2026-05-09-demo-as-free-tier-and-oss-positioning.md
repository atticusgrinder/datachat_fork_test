The standalone `/demo` page is gone. Every signed-up user now gets a read-only "Demo: RetailFlow" warehouse attached to their account on first login — they hit the full chat experience immediately, and can add their own warehouse alongside the demo. The demo connection can't be deleted. The landing page picks up an open-source pill, GitHub link, and a "run it yourself" callout under pricing — Datachat is going open source under Apache 2.0.
