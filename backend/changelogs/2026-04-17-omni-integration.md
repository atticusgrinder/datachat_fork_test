Connect your Omni BI project repo alongside (or instead of) dbt. View, model, and topic YAML files sync as context files, giving Claude the same rich understanding of your semantic layer that it gets from dbt — business definitions, relationships, and metrics all come through. Integration filenames also got a cleanup pass to remove redundant type prefixes and suffixes.
